I hold a doctorate degree in analytical chemistry and have over 30 years of experience with Rocky Mountain Instrumental Laboratories (RMIL). I have been accepted as an expert in the fields of analytical chemistry, drug testing, and forensic toxicology related to alcohol, cannabis, and illicit drugs as well as prescription medications. I have been qualified in courts throughout the State of Colorado, in various State courts and in Federal Courts Martial since 1989. Based on my education, experience and a successful grade on the qualifying exam I have been accepted as a Fellow by the American Board of Forensic Toxicology.
